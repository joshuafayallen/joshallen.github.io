Some Things I’ve Built

  • Featured Project

    Modeling Receiver Ability Bayesianly

    Isolating a player's skill from their surroundings is quite difficult since their are a variety potentially confounding team level variables. I built a Bayesian Hierarchical Factor model to estimate latent ability of every NFL pass catcher from 2002-present controlling for these team level factors and use Hilbert-Space Gaussian processes to capture in season time dynamics and career aging curves.

  • Featured Project

    NFL Power Rankings

    Who is the best team in the NFL? This is one of the most hotly debated topics throughout the NFL season. One of the big reasons this is so difficult is that not every team plays eachother. I alleviate these concerns by building a Bayesian paired-comparision model to answer these questions. You can see all these rankings by navigating to the webapp.

They are further constrained by the players they have available, game script, and the strengths of their opponents.
