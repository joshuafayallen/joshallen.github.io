Who is the best team in the NFL? This is one of the most hotly debated topics throughout the NFL season. One of the big reasons this is so difficult is that not every team plays eachother. I alleviate these concerns by building a Bayesian paired-comparision model to answer these questions. You can see all these rankings by navigating to the webapp.
